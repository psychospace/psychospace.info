**1275 PT** is a [[Year (unit)|year]] in [[Pinwheel Tempo]]. It took place in the [[13th century PT]]. 

# Events
- An elderly [[Hieron Dredge]] catches [[taphrine]], and has days to live. With nothing left to lose, he makes a power-play to drag [[Igor Ingolstadt]] out of hiding.

![[⎋ Uncommon#^1bfe2a]]
- A [[tailed stranger]] is sent back in time to [[Zero Day]], wielding an [[RDM-8]]. The travel is a success, and they return to 1275 having elucidated their own ability. However, the stranger withholds that their travel [[⎊ The Stranger's words|was a success]], in an attempt to take back their autonomy.
