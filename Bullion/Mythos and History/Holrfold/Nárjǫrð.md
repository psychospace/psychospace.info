**Nárjǫrð** (*nar-yarth*) is an elder [[earth]] [[Elemental creatures|elemental]] who was the tribal deity of [[Holrfold]]. 

[[Scribe adder|Alpoa-Derya wyrms]] were believed to be its limbs.
