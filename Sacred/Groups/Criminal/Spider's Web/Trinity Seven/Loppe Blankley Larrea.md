**Loppe Blankley Larrea** is a [[Elf#Shadar'kai|shadar'kai]] man, one of the [[Trinity Seven]], presiding over the Tangle of the [[Spider's Web]].

Called "Moon Rabbit", Loppe is one of the more esoteric members.
