The **Sienn Byway** is a span bridging the Southern [[Rainbow Ridge]] region in [[High Sacred]] to the [[Precipice Plains]] in [[Low Sacred]]. It is one of eight bridges between the [[remnant|remnants]] of the [[Kingdom of Sacred]].

This most ancient bridge is forged of [[Chronolite]], an ancient-tech mineral which under certain conditions can be "frozen in time". This makes it impossible to destroy it forever without dispelling the conditions it was put under in the first place. It was created by [[Installation S.H.O.R.E.]], now where the small trading settlement of [[Shero's Reach]].
