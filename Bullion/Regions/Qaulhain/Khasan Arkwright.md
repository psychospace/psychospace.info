**Khasan Artemios Arkwright** is a [[Gnome|gnomish]] emir of [[Emirate of Qaulhain|Qaulhain]], and, presently, the most wealthy man in [[Bullion]], along with the entirety of [[House Arkwright]]. 

Khasan is a baron whose company, the [[Lainese Heavy Industries]], is leading the modernization and redevelopment of the remnant (after the events of the [[Great Hurricane]]), alongside local companies and contractors.
